Senior Capstone Experience Reflection

This project presented a significant learning opportunity and a difficult learning curve. I’ve navigated through QGIS complexities and the inconsistencies of public geospatial data. I encountered several limitations, specifically the lack of fiber data from private providers. This information remains confidential. If I were to improve this project, we would seek to incorporate 3d topographic data to better simulate wind and heavy rain. Additionally, I would attempt to create something that could automate the mapping process and make it have real-time updates.
